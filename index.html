About Us

Importing food into the U.S. is complicated. We make it seamless.

With more than 50 years of combined experience and three licensed customs brokers, Agiloc International, Inc. has the knowledge to assure a smooth importation — holding your hand every step of the way.

We handle compliance, rules and regulations across every government agency involved in your shipment. Our in-depth understanding lets us anticipate compliance issues before importation, preventing complicated challenges at the port.

  • Full-agency compliance CBP, FDA, USDA, NOAA, Fish & Wildlife and more — managed end to end.
  • Permits & licenses fluency Agriculture Permits, IFTP, SIMP, Fish & Wildlife permits, VS16-3 and many more.
  • Post-clearance problem solving FDA detentions, relabeling, destructions, re-exportations and notice responses.
  • Door-to-door assistance Upon request, we support the process from manufacturer to final destination.
